About Little Tikes Daycare

Little Tikes Daycare is an in-home daycare that is owned by April Dillwith. The daycare is operated by her and her dedicated employees. The kids participate in learning activities everyday after breakfast and they start off their learning activities by reciting the Pledge of Allegiance every morning. On occasion, the kids will do arts and crafts and outside play when weather permits. The daycare provides breakfast, lunch, and afternoon snacks. After lunch the kids get ready for quiet time/nap time from 12-2. The daycare also has many learning toys and activities for the kids to participate in.

Why Choose Little Tikes?

Encouraged Learning

Learning is encouraged through activities, toys, and the environment.

School Drop-off and Pick-up

We have a drop-off and pick-up program in place for school-age children.

Well-Rounded Meals and Snacks

We provide a balanced breakfast and lunch with an additional afternoon snack.
